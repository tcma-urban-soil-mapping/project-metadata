  • Federal definition of Twin Cities metropolitan statistical area boundary
  • Minnesota Geological Survey county soil maps
  • Metropolitan Council/MetroGIS land use and land cover data
  • LiDAR digital elevation data
  • NRCS SSURGO soils data
  • Historic Twin Cities soil survey maps/data (for example, UMN Extension Bulletin 320 (1967))
  • Minnesota Geospatial Commons data, listed below:
    • TCMA 1-Meter Land Cover Classification
    • Counties and Cities & Townships, Twin Cities Metropolitan Area
    • Regional Parks and Trails - 2023 Annual Update
    • Generalized Land Use 2020
    • Lakes and Rivers - Open Water Features
    • Localized Flood Map for Climate Vulnerability Screening
    • Census 2020 Geography - Blocks, Block Groups, Tracts, Counties, and County Subdivisions
    • Census 2020 Geographic Crosswalks
    • Digital Orthoimagery, Twin Cities, Minnesota, Spring 2020, 1-ft Resolution
    • Counties and Cities & Townships 2020, Twin Cities Metropolitan Area
    • Generalized Land Use Historical (1984, 1990, 1997, 2000, 2005, 2010, 2016, 2020)
    • Metropolitan Urban Service Areas - Historical (1995 - 1998)
    • 2000 Urbanized Area and Urban Clusters
    • 1945 and 1959 Residential Land Use
    • 1990 to 2000 Census Tract Correspondence and Combined Tracts
    • Metropolitan Council Base Map Service
    • Annual Small Area Population and Household Estimates, Twin Cities Metropolitan Area
    • Twin Cities Metropolitan Area Regional Groundwater Flow Model, Version 2.00 (Metro Model 2) Projected Hydraulic Head DEMs - Twin Cities Metropolitan Area, Minnesota
    • Top of Bedrock Units
    • TIGER/Line Shapefile, 2019, nation, U.S., Current Metropolitan Statistical Area/Micropolitan Statistical Area (CBSA) National
    • D-01 Surficial Geology WFL1 (MGS)
    • Minnesota Geological Survey
    • Depth to bedrock (MGS)
    • NRCS gSSURGO database
    • DEM LiDAR
    • Maplewood Neighborhood Preserves
    • City of Blaine Open Space
